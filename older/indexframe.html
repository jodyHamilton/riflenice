More proof that Rifle Nice is preggo with an unreleased album. And for the superfans, you can now visualize which songs will be on it.

'Come by it Naturally' was originally a really mediocre guitar song I played with Steve and Pete Gavin years ago during some heavy drinking sessions. Then it improved somewhat into a keyboard jam on my second 'album.' Soon after, Alicia drew an accompanying picture book. This is all way back when. There was a child's desk (currently residing in my attic) where one could sit and examine the book while listening to the song on attached headphones. There was also an 'amusing' multiple choice exam one could take during or after studying the 'C b i N' experience (whereabouts currently unknown, however there is a new online personality quiz you can take instead). All of this was part of a show best described as the fringe of the fringe of the fringe festival, dark days indeed. With the formation of Rifle Nice in early 2004 the song was immediately revived and became an RN staple, although the words changed a little due to forgetting them. And it now features Alicia and Jim playing the mother and father where previously I would have to throw my voice. After many years missing, Alicia's picture book too has been recovered and I have now roughly animated it to make it a little more '2006.' I have to admit after all of this I still do not really have the words straight yet.
